Hamburg is the second-largest city in Germany and a major European tech hub.
The RAG Knowledge Assistant ingests plain-text documents, splits them into
chunks, embeds each chunk into a vector, and retrieves the most relevant
passages to ground an LLM's answer. This reduces hallucination because the
model answers only from retrieved context. The system supports OpenAI
embeddings and generation, and falls back to an offline hashing embedding and
extractive answering when no API key is present.
